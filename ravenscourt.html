Pebble Lyrics




Anyone who has ever travelled the coastline of Ireland will tell you of the Magic of our shores!

I went in search of said magic to create a very special picture for none other than renowned Irish chef, food author, tv personality and co founder of Ballymaloe Cookery school, Darina Allen who recently celebrated a very special birthday.

With relish I took the commission and set out to a locality quite near the lovely lady's home, Ballywilling Beach. The beaches of East cork unfold one after another to reveal spectacular land and seascapes and knowing that this lovely lady loves nothing better than to forage and source all her ingredients either from her own gardens and farm, or as local as can be, I thought it fitting her gift should be approached the same way.

I'm happy to say that that down to the seaweed frill on her skirt, the picture was a homegrown success! Hats off to the lady who commissioned the picture. She knew the way to Darina's heart!
