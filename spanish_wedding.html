Their perfect Day!

Some time back I was asked to make a wedding picture for a couple who were married in the beautiful region of Mijas in Spain. At the time I didn't realise that the stunning bride was actually an old friend of mine. There was double delight, for me when I realised who she was and for her when she received her gift!

The most interesting detail I received about the wedding was that the brides bouquet was made from brooches! Absolutely gorgeous and so unique! It made for a really special memento of the day and now the bouquet and picture sit side by side in pride of place.
