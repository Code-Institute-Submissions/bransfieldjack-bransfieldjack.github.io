C.O.S. There's always a shot, you just have to find it...

Some time back, I shared a very special picture, for a very special man, my Dad. As a result, another special man, who shares the same love of snooker and billiards that my Dad does, saw the picture and loved it. Took time out to visit my Pebble Lyrics page and like it. It was a big compliment from this Italian gentleman, who I've never met before.

Although the game is played similar, there are some differences. Skittles (birilli) feature in the Italian version but the principle is somewhat the same. So, I was quite thrilled to receive a commission from this gentleman's lovely daughter, to create a similar piece to that of my own fathers but with the Italian version represented. Even more thrilled to receive the evidence of his delight in his gift.

Pride of place!
